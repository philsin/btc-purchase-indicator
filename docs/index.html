Bitcoin Power Law Indicator

The Power Law: Bitcoin's price follows P = 10^a t^b where b 5.8. On a log-log chart, this forms a straight line.

log(Price) = a + b log(years_since_genesis)

Position (p): Location within the power law corridor (0-100%). Low = undervalued, high = overvalued.

Oscillator: Normalized log-deviation from trend (-1 to +1). All 4 historical ATHs occurred at 0.8-0.9.

Composite Signal: 60% Oscillator + 40% Position. Removes noise from liquidity/halving timing.

Fourier Wave: Fundamental mode from Perrenod's analysis, projecting ~2027 cycle peak.

Sources: Santostasi (2014), Burger (2019), Perrenod (2024)
